FAQ

Questions

Is this safe to use?

Yes. Relister runs entirely in your own browser using your logged-in Facebook session — the same actions you'd take manually, just faster. We never see your password, and nothing is stored on our servers.

Will relisting affect my buyers or messages?

Relisting creates a fresh copy of a listing and removes the old one. Existing conversations stay in your inbox. We recommend not relisting an item with active negotiations mid-deal.

Do you store my data?

No. Everything is processed locally in your browser. The only optional exception is the AI listing enhancement feature, which sends the listing text to Google Gemini using your own API key.

Is this an official Facebook/Meta product?

No. Marketplace Relister is an independent tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Meta Platforms, Inc. "Facebook" and "Marketplace" are trademarks of Meta.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Free lets you relist your 4 most-recent listings with a single click — great for casual sellers. Pro unlocks unlimited listings, bulk multi-select, AI listing enhancement, auto-relist scheduling (pick a date and time), and price-drop automation.

Can I try Pro before paying?

Yes — install free and explore the extension. Upgrade to Pro when you're ready to unlock bulk relisting, AI enhancement, and auto-scheduling.
