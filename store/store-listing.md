## Detailed Description

```
FB Marketplace Relister — refresh your listings to the top in seconds.

Facebook Marketplace listings drop in search visibility as they age. FB Marketplace Relister lets you relist any of your active items — carrying over every detail — so they appear fresh and near the top of local search again.

── FEATURES ──

⚡ 1-Click Relist
Select any listing from your Selling page and hit Relist. The extension reads your listing, re-uploads photos, and creates a fresh copy — carrying title, description, photos, category, price, location, and item condition. Old listing deleted automatically.

📦 Bulk Relist
Select multiple listings and relist them all at once. Each is processed sequentially with clear progress status shown in the popup.

💸 Price Drop on Relist
Apply a percentage or fixed-amount price reduction when relisting. Great for items you want to move quickly.

⏰ Scheduled Auto-Relist
Set a recurring schedule (e.g. every 12 hours) and let the extension relist your items automatically in the background while you get on with your day.

✨ AI Description Enhance (Optional)
Optionally improve your listing description using Google Gemini — powered by your own Google API key. Rewrites your description to be clearer and more compelling before relisting. You control it; we never see your data.

── HOW IT WORKS ──

1. Go to your Facebook Marketplace Selling page.
2. Click the Relist button that appears on any listing, or open the extension popup to bulk-select.
3. The extension reads your listing data directly from the page, re-uploads your photos to Facebook's servers, and creates a new listing via Facebook's API.
4. Your old listing is deleted. Your refreshed listing now appears near the top of search.

No UI automation — no fake clicks through Facebook's forms. The extension calls Facebook's own internal API directly from your already-logged-in browser session.

── PRIVACY ──

Everything runs locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server we operate. The only optional external call is to Google Gemini (if you enable AI enhance) using your own API key. No analytics, no tracking, no data sold.

```
