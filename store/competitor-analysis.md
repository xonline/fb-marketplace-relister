## 5. Gap Analysis and Positioning

### Where Every Competitor Falls Short

**Relistify (the market leader, 603 users, 3.9★):**
- Price drop requires a $3.33/mo subscription — we offer it free
- No scheduled auto-relist — we schedule recurring relists in the background
- No AI description enhancement
- Condition carry-over not mentioned — we explicitly carry over item condition
- Despite being the "leader", only 3.9 stars — reviews cite reliability issues

**Marketplace Listings Reposter (largest base, 993 users, 2.7★):**
- Worst satisfaction score of any competitor (2.7★ from 35 ratings)
- No price drop, no scheduling, no AI, no condition carry-over
- Their 993 users are a prime acquisition target — low bar to outperform

**AutoListing (closest analog at $2/mo, 33 users, 4.0★):**
- 5 relists/month on free tier — we are effectively unlimited
- No price drop, no scheduling, no condition carry-over, no AI
- Tiny install base — just entered the market

**Facebook Marketplace Automator FMA (347 users, unrated):**
- No ratings at all — new or abandoned
- Uses "spreadsheet data storage" approach which is clunky for most sellers
- Free but feature-poor

### Our Uncontested Differentiators

1. **Item condition carry-over** — Every competitor ignores this field. Buyers filter by condition; losing it on relist hurts conversions. We're the only one that preserves it.
2. **Scheduled auto-relist in background (free)** — Only FMA attempts scheduling; ours runs as a browser alarm without requiring the user to be present. Set it, forget it.
3. **Price drop on relist (free tier)** — Relistify charges $3.33/mo for this. We give it free. This is a direct conversion lever vs the market leader.
4. **AI enhancement with YOUR own key** — No competitor offers AI. We do — and it's zero-trust: the user's own Gemini API key, no data touches our servers. Unique privacy story.
5. **No external server dependency** — Everything runs locally in the browser. Some competitors (FMA, Marketplace Listing Tool) show signs of server-side processing. We can truthfully say "no account required, no cloud, no subscription needed."

### Positioning Statement

**"The only Facebook Marketplace relister that schedules automatically, drops prices in one step, and carries over item condition — all free, all in your browser. No subscriptions for core features. No data leaves your machine."**
