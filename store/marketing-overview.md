## Detailed Description (final, ready to paste)

```
Relist for Facebook Marketplace — refresh your listings to the top in seconds.

Marketplace listings drop in search visibility as they age. Relist for Facebook Marketplace deletes your old listing and recreates it as new — carrying over every detail including title, description, photos, category, price, location, and item condition — so it appears fresh and near the top of local search again.

No subscriptions required for core features. No account to create. No data sent to any server we operate. Everything runs locally in your browser.

── FEATURES ──

⚡ One-Click Relist
A Relist button appears directly on your Facebook Marketplace Selling page. Click it on any listing — the extension reads your listing data, re-uploads your photos, creates a fresh copy, and deletes the old one. Done in under 10 seconds.

📦 Bulk Relist
Select multiple listings in the popup and relist them all at once. Each is processed in sequence with real-time progress shown per item.

💸 Price Drop on Relist (FREE)
Apply a percentage or fixed-amount price reduction when relisting. Great for shifting items that have sat too long. Competing extensions charge a monthly subscription for this — we include it free.

🏷️ Item Condition Carry-Over
Your listing's condition (New, Like New, Good, Fair, Poor) is preserved on every relist. No other relister on the Chrome Web Store does this — buyers filter by condition, and losing it costs you sales.

⏰ Scheduled Auto-Relist (FREE)
Set a recurring interval (every 12 hours, every 24 hours) and the extension relists your items automatically in the background using your existing browser session — no click required. Competing extensions require you to be present.

✨ AI Description Enhance (Optional)
Optionally improve your listing description using Google Gemini before relisting. Powered by your own Google API key — no key of ours is involved, no data is logged. Rewrites your copy to be cleaner and more compelling.

── HOW IT WORKS ──

1. Go to your Facebook Marketplace Selling page.
2. Click the Relist button on any listing — or open the popup to select multiple.
3. The extension reads your listing data directly from the page and re-uploads your photos to Facebook's own servers.
4. A fresh listing is created via Facebook's API. The old listing is deleted.
5. Your refreshed listing appears near the top of local search with a new timestamp.

No fake mouse clicks through Facebook's forms. The extension calls Facebook's internal API directly from your already-logged-in browser session — the same approach your browser uses when you click anything on Marketplace.

── PRIVACY ──

Everything runs locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server we operate. The only optional external call is to Google Gemini (when you enable AI Enhance) using your own API key. No analytics. No tracking. No data sold. No account required.
```
