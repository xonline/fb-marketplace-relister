# Promo Video Script — Relist for Facebook Marketplace

*30–45 second screen recording. Doable solo with OBS or Loom + basic caption overlay. No voiceover required — captions carry it.*

---

## Format

- **Type:** Screen recording with caption overlays
- **Length:** ~38 seconds (as scripted)
- **Audio:** Optional background music (upbeat, minimal — e.g. lofi house or corporate upbeat. No voiceover needed.)
- **Captions:** White text, bold, centered bottom-third. Each shown 3–4 seconds.
- **Resolution:** 1280×720 minimum (16:9). Record at 1280×800 to match screenshots if possible.

---

## Scene-by-Scene Script

---

### SCENE 1 — The Problem (0:00–0:06)

**Visual:**
Facebook Marketplace `/marketplace/you/selling` page. Scroll slowly down a list of listings. One listing shows a timestamp: "Listed 7 days ago — 3 views."

Pause on it. The low view count and stale timestamp are visible.

**Caption overlay:**
> "Your Facebook Marketplace listings go stale fast."

**Duration:** 6 seconds

---

### SCENE 2 — The Solution (0:06–0:11)

**Visual:**
Mouse hovers over a "Relist" button that appears inline next to the listing (injected by the extension). The button glows or has a subtle hover state.

Mouse clicks it.

**Caption overlay:**
> "One click — and it's back at the top."

**Duration:** 5 seconds

---

### SCENE 3 — The Process (0:11–0:18)

**Visual:**
Extension popup animates open. Shows real-time progress steps ticking through:
- ✓ Reading listing...
- ✓ Uploading photos...
- ✓ Creating listing...
- ✓ Done

A green checkmark animates in. Total elapsed time visible: "8 seconds."

**Caption overlay (first 3s):**
> "Photos, price, condition — all carried over."

**Caption overlay (last 4s, on the Done screen):**
> "Done in under 10 seconds."

**Duration:** 7 seconds

---

### SCENE 4 — Bulk Relist (0:18–0:24)

**Visual:**
Extension popup showing the listing selector. Four listings get quickly checked off (each checkbox clicks visibly with a tick animation). The "Relist 4 Selected" button appears and is clicked.

Progress starts — four items process one after another with small checkmarks.

**Caption overlay:**
> "Or select multiple and bulk relist them all at once."

**Duration:** 6 seconds

---

### SCENE 5 — Auto-Schedule (0:24–0:30)

**Visual:**
Cut to the Options page. Mouse clicks the "Enable scheduled auto-relist" toggle — it turns green. The interval dropdown shows "Every 12 hours."

Mouse moves to close the tab.

**Caption overlay (first 3s):**
> "Set a schedule — relists happen automatically in the background."

**Caption overlay (last 3s — show a clock icon animation or the toggle staying ON):**
> "Even while you sleep."

**Duration:** 6 seconds

---

### SCENE 6 — Value Hook (0:30–0:35)

**Visual:**
Cut back to the Marketplace search results page. The same listing from Scene 1 now shows "Listed just now" and appears near the top of the grid, highlighted with a brief glow or upward motion.

**Caption overlay:**
> "Free. No account. No subscription for core features."

**Duration:** 5 seconds

---

### SCENE 7 — CTA (0:35–0:38)

**Visual:**
Chrome Web Store listing page for the extension. Mouse clicks "Add to Chrome."

A simple graphic fades in:
- Extension icon (large, centered)
- Text below: "Relist for Facebook Marketplace"
- Subline: "Free on the Chrome Web Store"

**Caption overlay:**
> "Get it free on the Chrome Web Store."

**Duration:** 3 seconds

## Production Checklist

- [ ] Use a real Facebook account (a test/dummy seller account is fine — no real items needed)
- [ ] Pre-load 4–5 test listings with distinct product photos before recording
- [ ] Record at 1280×720 or 1280×800 in Chrome with the extension loaded
- [ ] Use OBS for recording + SimpleScreenRecorder as a fallback
- [ ] Add captions in DaVinci Resolve (free), CapCut, or Canva Video
- [ ] Caption font: Bold sans-serif (DM Sans Bold or Montserrat Black). White text, 2px black outline for readability on any background.
- [ ] Background music: BeatPack or YouTube Audio Library. Track: upbeat, under 60 BPM to feel controlled, not frantic.
- [ ] Export: MP4, H.264, 1280×720 minimum, under 100MB (Chrome Web Store limit)
- [ ] Do NOT include any personal user data, real names, real addresses, or real phone numbers in any listing shown on screen
